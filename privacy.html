Your privacy matters to us. SQUIDS is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the SQUIDS platform, accessible via squids.co.za, app.squids.co.za, and associated mobile applications and services.

By using SQUIDS, you consent to the processing of your personal information as described in this policy. This policy must be read in conjunction with our Terms of Service and Cookie Policy.

2

Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account registration data: username, display name, email address, password (hashed).
  • Profile information: bio, avatar, banner image, website URL.
  • Content you create: posts, comments, questions, replies, and direct messages.
  • Communication with support: emails and in-app support requests.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type, operating system.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy).

2.3 Information from third parties

  • Authentication providers if you use social login.
  • AI service providers (prompts and interaction data).
3

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information to:

  • Operate and improve the SQUIDS platform and its features.
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • Deliver notifications, messages, and platform updates.
  • Provide AI-powered features and personalised content recommendations.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with our legal obligations under South African law.
  • Analyse usage trends to improve platform performance.
  • Send service communications (not marketing, unless you opt in).
4

Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA), we process your data on the following lawful grounds:

  • Consent — you have given us consent to process your personal information for a specific purpose.
  • Contract — processing is necessary to fulfil our contractual obligations under the Terms of Service.
  • Legitimate interests — processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation — processing is required to comply with South African law.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at legal@squids.co.za. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — hosting, analytics, and infrastructure partners who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • AI providers — when you use AI features, prompts may be processed by third-party AI providers.
  • Law enforcement — when legally required by South African courts or statutory authority.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality obligations.

All third-party processors are contractually required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

6

AI Services & Third-Party Processors

When you interact with AI features on SQUIDS, your prompts and interactions may be sent to third-party AI providers for processing. These providers have their own privacy policies and terms.

We recommend reviewing the terms of any AI providers before submitting sensitive personal information in AI prompts. AI providers process data solely for service delivery and safety purposes.

See: Terms §7: AI Services

Your Rights Under POPIA

Under POPIA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access
Request a copy of your personal information we hold.
Right to Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data where legally permissible.
Right to Object
Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
Right to Portability
Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
Right to Complain
Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
9

How to Submit a POPIA Data Request

To exercise any of your POPIA rights, send a written request to our Information Officer:

Information Officer — SQUIDS
Email: legal@squids.co.za
Subject line: "POPIA Data Request — [Your Username]"
Please include: your registered email, the nature of your request, and proof of identity.

We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and respond fully within 30 days as required by POPIA.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforegulator.org.za.

10

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Hashed and salted password storage — we never store passwords in plain text.
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data.
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring.

In the event of a data breach that may prejudice you, we will notify you and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA.

11

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your active account and provide services.
  • Comply with legal retention obligations under South African law.
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security.

After account deletion, most personal data is deleted within 90 days, except where legal obligations require longer retention. Some anonymised usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytics purposes.

See: Terms §20: Data Retention
12

Children's Privacy

SQUIDS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at legal@squids.co.za and we will promptly delete it.

Users aged 13–17 must have parental consent to use SQUIDS. See our Terms §4: Eligibility for details.

The latest version is always available at policies.squids.co.za/privacy.
