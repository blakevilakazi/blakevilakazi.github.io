WHAT ARE COOKIES? Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow websites to: recognise your device, remember preferences, maintain login sessions, improve performance, analyse usage, enable advertising, secure services. Cookies do not normally access files on your device, but may collect identifiers such as IP address, browser type, or device information. 4.
