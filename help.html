Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create a SQUIDS account?
Visit app.squids.co.za and click "Sign Up". Enter your email, choose a unique username, set a display name, and create a password. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your email.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Go to the forgot password page, enter your registered email, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't receive it within 5 minutes.
How do I change my username?
Go to Settings → Account in the app. You can change your username once, and it must be unique. Your profile URL will update automatically.
Is the AI chatbot safe to use?
Yes, but always apply critical thinking. AI responses are generated automatically and may be inaccurate. They are not professional advice. See our AI Services terms for full details.
How do I report abuse or harmful content?
Use the report button on any post, comment, or profile in the app. For urgent cases, email support@squids.co.za with "URGENT" in the subject. See our Community Guidelines: Reporting for details.
How is my data protected?
SQUIDS is fully compliant with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act). Your data is encrypted, never sold, and you have the right to access, correct, or delete it. See our Privacy Policy.
My account was suspended. How do I appeal?
Email support@squids.co.za within 14 days of suspension with the subject "Account Appeal — [your username]". Include your reasoning and any relevant context. All appeals are reviewed fairly.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone in the app and click "Delete Account". This is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data is deleted within 90 days per our Privacy Policy.
What age do I need to be to use SQUIDS?
You must be at least 13 years old. Users aged 13–17 require parental or guardian consent. See Terms §4: Eligibility for full requirements.
How do I submit a POPIA data request?
Email legal@squids.co.za with subject "POPIA Data Request — [your username]". We respond within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy §9 for details.
